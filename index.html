IT Instructor - Information Technology - Web Developer

I have gained extensive experience as a web developer through my participation in Skills Competitions organized by TESDA and World Skills Philippines. In 2021, I competed in the Web Technologies category, where I earned a gold medal in both the District and Regional Skills Competitions and a bronze medal at the Philippine National Skills Competition.


In 2022, I was promoted to the role of Web Technology Expert. In this capacity, I have been actively involved in facilitating Web Technology Skills Competitions and coaching competitors. My guidance has led to gold medal victories for my trainees in the 2022 District and Regional Competitions, as well as the 2023 Regional Skills Competition. Currently, I am serving as a delegate and expert for the 2024 Philippine National Skills Competition.

For the past two years, I have dedicated myself to teaching, training, supervising, and mentoring youth and students who aspire to become IT professionals and programmers.
